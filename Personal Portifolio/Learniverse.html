Learniverse

Project Description:

Learniverse is a comprehensive platform where users can learn, take exams, manage to-do lists, take notes, access study material, publish questionnaires, add questions, and provide feedback. Many students in our country struggle with exams due to a lack of resources and mental focus.

Learniverse addresses these issues by offering academic materials and self- development books to strengthen their mindset.

Additionally, the platform helps teachers with time management and simplifies the process of creating and managing surveys for researchers, saving time and reducing costs.

Since this website won the state tech and science competition, it has gained recognition and is currently undergoing maintenance. It will be deployed soon and available for all students, teachers, and researchers. Learniverse is set to revolutionize the current system.
